About us...

Hello everyone! We are a German American family, who have lived in the Germany for over 30 years, and also, this is a picture of our dog! He also says hi.

We both love to test out new ideas and materials and have a wide range of light and dark humor which you can see in most of the products we create. We appreciate our quiet private life but we enjoy sharing ideas and laughs with our customers.

We have the ability to create reproduction of the same item or a one of a kind custom piece. If you need a gift or have a project idea that you would like to explore, feel free to get in touch with us!
